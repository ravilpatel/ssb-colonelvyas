What is SRT?

SRT stands for Situation Reaction Test. You need to react on given situation. For example, the situation given is: He saw someone smoking in public. He...

You need to write the answer after "HE". The answer could be, He asked him not to smoke in public and instead smoke in private.


Important points to note

  • A total of 60 situations will be given to the candidates and they have to respond to it within 30 minute time limit.
  • There will be timer in left-bottom to be used with timing of 30 min.
  • You can download the pdf and attempt it after printing it out (Recommended)
